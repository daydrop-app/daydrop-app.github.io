Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Your privacy is the foundation of Daydrop. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Journal Data

Your journal entries are encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM encryption before being stored locally. Your journal text is never transmitted to our servers. We cannot read your journal entries.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information (email address) for authentication
  • Entry metadata (date, mood/vibe, word count) — never the text content
  • App usage analytics to improve the product (anonymized)
  • Crash reports to fix bugs (no journal content included)
  • Subscription status via RevenueCat

3. AI Features

When you use AI Reflect or AI Insights, your journal text is processed locally before being sent to our AI service. We use Anthropic's Claude API. AI interactions are not stored permanently and are not used to train AI models.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase (authentication and metadata storage)
  • Anthropic (AI features, processed locally first)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • PostHog (anonymized analytics)

5. Data Retention

Account metadata is retained while your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the app settings. Journal data exists only on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Daydrop is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at hstransit1@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app.

9. Contact

Questions? Email us at hstransit1@gmail.com
